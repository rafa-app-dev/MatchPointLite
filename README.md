# MatchPointLite

The ultimate scoring assistant for Padel and Tennis on Apple Watch.

## Support
If you have any questions, issues, or feedback, please contact us through this repo.

## Privacy Policy
**MatchPointLite** respects your privacy. 
* **Data Collection:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
* **Data Usage:** All match scores and history are stored locally on your Apple Watch.
* **Third Parties:** No data is shared with third parties.
